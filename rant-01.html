NOBODY ACTUALLY READS THE SECURITY POLICY

Hot take incoming... you've been warned.

Let me say what everyone in infosec already knows but won't say out loud: nobody reads the security policy.

Not the employees. Not the managers. Honestly? Sometimes not even the people who wrote it.

So Why Do We Keep Writing Them?

It's a compliance checkbox. A 47-page PDF that lives in a SharePoint folder nobody visits, written in language designed to satisfy an auditor — not to actually change behaviour.

"But we send it to all new hires during onboarding!"
Yeah. Right before they set up their laptop and forget it immediately.

What Actually Works

  • Short, specific, plain-English guidelines
  • Repeat them constantly — in Slack, in standups, everywhere
  • Make the secure thing the easy thing

Policies don't change culture. People do. End of rant.
